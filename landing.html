The Missing Artwork

Your chance to find the Missing piece of Artwork

Welcome to the Museum of Fine Art! Unfortunately, we have some bad news to share. Last night, a group of thieves broke into the museum and stole one of our most prized possessions - a rare and valuable painting by an unknown artist.

We need your help to solve this crime and recover the missing artwork. We've gathered some clues that we believe will lead you to the painting's whereabouts. But be warned, this is no easy task. You'll need to use your wits and powers of deduction to uncover each clue and follow the trail to the painting.

Your journey starts in the Van Gogh room. Look for the painting that is missing from its frame, and you'll be on your way to cracking this case.
